Our Foundation

Our Foundation

Our History

Founded in 2009, Milestone School began as a modest educational initiative with a clear vision: to create a place where every learner could grow academically, socially, and morally. Over the years, it has evolved into a respected institution in Kabwe, known for blending modern teaching methods with strong traditional values.

From its early days, the school has upheld its guiding principle of "Humble Grooming for a Bright Future." What started as a small community school has now become a vibrant learning center that nurtures curiosity, discipline, and creativity in every pupil.

Our Mission

At Milestone School, our mission is to provide a holistic education that goes beyond academic success. We strive to cultivate intellectual curiosity, resilience, and empathy in our learners. Through a supportive environment and dedicated teachers, we aim to equip each child with the knowledge, skills, and character needed to thrive in an ever-changing world.

Our Vision

We aspire to be a leading educational institution in Central Province, recognized for our commitment to excellence, innovative teaching practices, and unwavering dedication to the success and well-being of every learner. Our vision extends beyond the classroom, we seek to prepare students who will not only excel in their careers but also contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and strong moral principles in all we do.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in academics and personal growth.
  • Respect: Fostering an environment of mutual understanding and kindness.
  • Discipline: Encouraging responsibility, focus, and perseverance.
  • Innovation: Embracing creativity and technology to enhance learning.

Our Commitment

Milestone School continues to build upon its legacy by integrating ICT education, arts, and character development into every level of learning. We take pride in shaping learners who are confident, capable, and ready to face the future with integrity and purpose.

Our Duties

At Milestone School, our duties focus on creating a nurturing environment, delivering high-quality education, ensuring student safety, and supporting academic and personal growth for every learner.

Our Programs

Academics

Overview of Curriculum

At Milestone School, our curriculum blends the strengths of the Zambian education system with the internationally recognized Cambridge framework. This integration ensures that our learners receive a well-rounded education that meets both local and global standards.

Through the Cambridge curriculum, we promote inquiry-based learning, critical thinking, and problem-solving. Subjects are taught using practical, engaging methods that encourage students to explore concepts deeply rather than rely on memorization.

By integrating Cambridge learning with local values and cultural understanding, Milestone School ensures that every learner is prepared to thrive in a connected and competitive world.

Teaching Methodologies

At Milestone School, teaching goes beyond textbooks. We believe that effective education combines knowledge, curiosity, and character development. Our approach centers on active learning, where students participate through discussion, experimentation, and problem-solving.

Teachers use a variety of modern methodologies such as project-based learning, inquiry-based instruction, and blended learning supported by technology. ICT is integrated across subjects, helping learners build digital literacy.

Through this balanced and forward-looking approach, Milestone School prepares every learner to become an informed, capable, and compassionate member of society.

Extracurricular Activities

At Milestone School, we believe that learning extends beyond the classroom. Our extracurricular program is designed to develop the whole child by nurturing talents, building confidence, and promoting teamwork.

Learners are encouraged to participate in a wide range of clubs and activities, including Music, Drama, Debate, Chess, and Art. Sports play a key role, fostering discipline, fitness, and school spirit.

Through this vibrant mix of academic and extracurricular experiences, Milestone School ensures that every learner grows intellectually, socially, and emotionally.

Learning Resources and Facilities

Milestone School provides a well-equipped and stimulating learning environment. Our ICT laboratory offers hands-on experience with computers and modern digital tools. Classrooms are spacious, well-lit, and equipped with up-to-date teaching materials.

The library, science laboratory, art studio, and sports grounds provide opportunities for practical learning, creative expression, and physical development.

Academic Achievements and Success Stories

Milestone School stands as a center of academic distinction in Kabwe, known for producing disciplined, confident, and high-performing learners. Our learners have consistently achieved top results in internal and external examinations.

We take pride in our graduates who have advanced to prestigious secondary schools, both locally and internationally, continuing to reflect the values instilled at Milestone, humility, discipline, and determination.
