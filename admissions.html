Our Admission Process

  1. 1
    Inquiry: Contact our administration office to express interest and ask any preliminary questions. We're happy to help!
  2. 2
    Application: Download and complete the application form. Submit it to our office along with the required documents.
  3. 3
    Assessment: Prospective students will be invited for a friendly, age-appropriate assessment to understand their learning level.
  4. 4
    Offer of Place: Successful applicants will receive an official offer of admission from the school.
  5. 5
    Enrollment: Complete enrollment by submitting all required documents and fees.

Required Documents

  • Completed Application Form
  • Copy of the child's birth certificate
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • Copy of previous school reports

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What curriculum do you follow? We blend the Zambian education system with the Cambridge framework.
  • What subjects are offered? English, Mathematics, Science, ICT, Arts, and a broader set of core learning areas.
  • How is progress assessed? Through continuous assessment, classwork, tests, and structured evaluations.
  • Do you support different learning needs? Yes, we provide individualized support and close learner monitoring.
